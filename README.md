# Total House Washing & Commercial - Image Assets

This project requires the following image assets to be placed in the `assets/images/` folder:

## Required Images

### Hero Section
- `hero-house-washing.jpg` - Wide residential house washing image showing a worker pressure washing a home with the house clearly visible

### Service Cards
- `house-washing-siding.jpg` - Image showing house siding being washed/cleaned
- `pressure-washing-driveway.jpg` - Image showing driveway or concrete pressure washing
- `roof-gutter-cleaning.jpg` - Image showing roof, gutter, or upper house cleaning with a ladder
- `deck-fence-washing.jpg` - Image showing deck or fence washing
- `commercial-building-cleaning.jpg` - Image showing storefront or commercial building cleaning

### Gallery Section
- `gallery-house-washing.jpg` - Residential house washing service in action
- `gallery-driveway-cleaning.jpg` - Driveway pressure washing before and after
- `gallery-roof-cleaning.jpg` - Professional roof cleaning service
- `gallery-deck-fence.jpg` - Deck and fence cleaning results
- `gallery-commercial.jpg` - Commercial building exterior cleaning
- `gallery-service-truck.jpg` - Professional service truck and equipment

### Featured Work/Video Section
- `service-truck-professional.jpg` - Professional service truck and equipment for house washing

### Background Images
- `reviews-background.jpg` - Background image for reviews section (can be a duplicate of hero image or similar professional image)

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Quality**: High resolution (at least 1200px wide for hero, 700px wide for service cards, 500px wide for gallery)
- **Aspect Ratios**:
  - Hero: Wide format (16:9 or similar)
  - Service cards: Square or 4:3
  - Gallery: 4:3 aspect ratio
- **Content**: Professional, clean images focused on pressure washing/house washing services
- **Alt Text**: All images have descriptive alt text for accessibility

## Notes

- All images should be optimized for web (compressed but high quality)
- Ensure images are not stretched or distorted
- Maintain consistent visual style across all images
- Images should convey professionalism and trust
